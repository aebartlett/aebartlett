A Brief History of my Life

Languages have always been one of my greatest interests and passions. I was born in Salzburg, Austria, where I was raised bilingually, speaking both German and English fluently. During my time at a language-focused middle and high school, I was lucky enough to study Latin, Ancient Greek, French, and Spanish. I developed a special interest in perfecting my Spanish, which led me to study abroad in Malaga, Spain, as well as to work and travel through Ecuador and Peru.

One of my goals coming into college was to learn another language, so I delved into Arabic. Recently, however, I have discovered that studying Computer Science is like learning a dozen languages at once, and I am excited to continue my journey of many languages in this field.
